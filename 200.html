Our Story

About Sonic Logistics

Founded by Suay Erol Selalmaz in Berlin-Charlottenburg, Sonic Logistics was born from a simple conviction: urban delivery doesn't have to come at the cost of our environment.

What started as a vision for cleaner city logistics has grown into a trusted delivery partner for major carriers — operating an all-electric Mercedes van fleet across Berlin's busiest postal codes.

We combine local expertise with sustainable innovation, proving that green logistics isn't just good for the planet — it's good for business.
